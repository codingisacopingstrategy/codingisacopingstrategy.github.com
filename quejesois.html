performance, 5 digital pictures, 2008

Photographie Meta Scheltes, Coiffure & Maquillage Esther Hardorff

‘That I am awesomely attractive and that I frequent the handsome and the hip, that is not what this is about.’

I have hired a makeup artist to tend to me before the opening of an exhibition. The makeup allows me to look natural where other faces get washed out bracing the flash of the photocamera. In this way I am more at home on the pictures of the opening than on the event itself.
