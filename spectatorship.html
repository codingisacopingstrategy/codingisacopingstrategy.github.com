Spectatorship

Dance-performance collaboration

Eric Schrijver was the resident artist in this performance by choreographer Bruno Listopad. He performed a monologue for emancipated spectators. The dancers engaged in a ‘making strange’, questioning whether passive spectatorship is actually possible. Spectatorship was produced by Korzo productions and toured the Netherlands.
