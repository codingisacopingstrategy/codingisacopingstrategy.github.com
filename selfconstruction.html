Selfconstruction as a methodology

“Writers like George Steiner had nailed the sexy term post-culture and it seemed a jolly good idea to join up the dots for rock. The main platform would be, other than shoes, “we are the future, now” and the one way of celebrating that was to create it by the only means at our disposal. With, of course, a rock ’n roll band”

Bowie as quoted in the: Notes from the guest artist

The performance-installation ‘self construction as a methodology’ came out of the research of choreographer Bruno Listopad at Danslab. Eric Schrijver participated in this research as the Guest Artist.

Parts of the material were subsequently brought into Spectatorship, a production for the stage.
