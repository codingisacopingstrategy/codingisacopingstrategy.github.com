Bristol Knowledge Unconference

Bristol Knowledge Unconference is an "Unconference" specifically about Knowledge. It takes place during Uncraftivism. This years theme is the ‘art of knowledge’. Most presentations are from scholars, many of which deal with the semantic web.

An impromptue talk about semantics.

Several interventions in the Arnolfini
