Nutshuis, The Hague

De Nieuwe Garde Den Haag Vraagt

June 24th, 2009

De Nieuwe Garde Den Haag hosts an evening of discussion.

Eric presents a récit on the artists task. How artists should aspire to bring their vision to the realm of economics and thereby changing what economics is. See also If you’re twenty-five you don’t wanna be alive.

In a panel he joins Ted Langenbach.
